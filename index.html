Are you ugly?

Both, cats and dogs do not mind the way you look. They love you as long as you feed them on a regular basis and cuddle them often. Big plus during winter time: they keep you warm!

Having a bad day?

It does not matter if you are happy smiling from one ear to the other or you are deep down in a depression. They love you anyway. As long as you do not go on vacation without them.

You like Honesty?

Even if those lovely creatures sometimes adopt human behaviour, one thing will never change: They are honest ALL the time. However, you need to know their body language.
